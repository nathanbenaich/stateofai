STATE OF AI REPORT 2018.

The State of AI Report analyses the most interesting developments in AI. We aim to trigger an informed conversation about the state of AI and its implication for the future. The Report is produced by AI investors Nathan Benaich of Air Street Capital and Ian Hogarth.

    In our inaugural 2018 report, we considered the following key dimensions in our report:
  1. Research: Technology breakthroughs and capabilities.
  2. Talent: Supply, demand and concentration of AI talent.
  3. Industry: Areas of commercial application for AI and its business impact.
  4. Politics: Regulation of AI, its economic implications and the emerging geopolitics of AI.
  5. Predictions: What we believe will happen and a performance review to keep us honest.
