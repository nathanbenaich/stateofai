State of AI

Artificial intelligence (AI) is a multidisciplinary field of science whose goal is to create intelligent machines.

We believe that AI is the most important lever for change in our increasingly digital, data-driven world. This is because everything around us today, ranging from culture to consumer products, is a product of intelligence.

In this report, we set out to capture a snapshot of the exponential progress in AI with a focus on developments in the past 12 months. Consider this report as a compilation, a mixtape of sorts, that seeks to trigger informed conversation about the state of AI and its implication for the future.

    We consider the following key dimensions in our report:
  1. Research: Technology breakthroughs and their capabilities.
  2. Talent: Supply, demand and forecasts for skilled human capital.
  3. Industry: Large platforms, financings and areas of application for AI-driven innovation today and tomorrow.
  4. China: Large platforms, financings and areas of application for AI-driven innovation today and tomorrow.
  5. Politics: AI Nationalism and the global power dynamics between nation states.
