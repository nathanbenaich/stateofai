State of AI Report Compute Index

The State of AI Report Compute Index tracks the size of public, private and national HPC clusters. As a key substrate upon which AI models are trained and run, the size of compute clusters helps us take a temperature check on the rate of progress of AI systems.


Notes: Public Cloud = capacity rented from hyperscalers; Private Cloud = owned and run by the company; National HPC = government owned and run.
