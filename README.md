# State of AI Report

The [State of AI Report](https://www.stateof.ai) is the most widely read and trusted independent, annual report on global progress in artificial intelligence. First published in 2018, it synthesizes key developments in AI research, industry, geopolitics, and safety to provide a long-term, systems-level view of how the field is evolving.

The report is edited and produced by [Nathan Benaich](https://www.twitter.com/nathanbenaich) in collaboration with contributors across research, industry, and policy, and is published by [Air Street Capital](https://www.airstreet.com).
